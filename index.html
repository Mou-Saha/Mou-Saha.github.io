Services

As a skilled BI/Data Analyst, I offer a range of services to help businesses harness the power of data and gain valuable insights. Through my expertise in data analysis, visualization, and reporting, I assist organizations in making data-driven decisions and optimizing their operations. Whether it's designing intuitive dashboards, developing robust data models, leveraging cloud databases, or understanding and translating business requirements, I provide comprehensive solutions to drive business growth and efficiency.

Reporting

In the realm of reporting, I excel in creating impactful and visually appealing reports that present complex data in a clear and concise manner. By utilizing tools like Power BI and advanced reporting techniques, I transform raw data into meaningful insights that enable stakeholders to make informed decisions. With a keen eye for detail and a focus on user experience, I ensure that my reports provide actionable information and contribute to the organization's overall data-driven strategy.

Data Modelling

Data modelling is a crucial aspect of my services, where I design and develop robust data models that lay the foundation for efficient data analysis and reporting. Through a deep understanding of business processes and data relationships, I create logical and physical data models that optimize data storage, retrieval, and manipulation. By leveraging industry best practices and employing tools like SQL and data modeling software, I ensure that the data models I build are scalable, flexible, and aligned with the organization's data needs.

Cloud Database

With the advent of cloud computing, I specialize in leveraging cloud databases to unlock the full potential of data. By utilizing platforms like Amazon Web Services (AWS) or Microsoft Azure, I help organizations store, manage, and analyze their data in a secure and scalable environment. I design cloud database architectures that ensure data integrity, availability, and performance, enabling businesses to harness the power of the cloud and derive valuable insights from their data.

Requirement Engineering

Understanding and translating business requirements is a key aspect of my services as a BI/Data Analyst. Through effective requirement engineering, I collaborate closely with stakeholders to identify their needs, goals, and challenges. I employ methodologies such as interviews, workshops, and analysis techniques to gather comprehensive requirements that serve as the foundation for data analysis and reporting solutions. By bridging the gap between business and technology, I ensure that the solutions I deliver align with the organization's strategic objectives and drive measurable results.
